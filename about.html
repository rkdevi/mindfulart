About Me

Strong and rich experience spanning over Twenty Three years of a Colourful journey with Art has made me realize that Colours can do Magic in each of our lives. To spread this, magic and happiness to all, I have worked out exclusive modules keeping in mind particular groups like Expectant Mothers, New Moms, Kids, Adults ,Senior Citizens, Special Children and Cancer patients.

The pursuit for specialized knowledge of Colours led me to ACC Institute of Human Service for an Art Therapy Course at Singapore. With this unique experience and knowledge gained from this Prestigious institution, I venture out to sow and multiply the happiness that Colours offer to humankind.

Twenty three years of knowledge sharing experience with Colours, Murals, Clay works, Acrylic Paintings, Coffee Paintings, Decoupage, Mixed media art works, Knife painting et al.

India’s rich heritage of Tribal and Folk Art forms have sparked a very keen interest in myself, inspiring me to learn the multitude of tribal and folk art forms leading to forming exclusive and specialized course curriculums for imparting the acquired knowledge to the diverse spectrum of students.

Have connected with more than 6400+ students through Colours and have been an instrumental part of engaging, understanding, uplifting their Colourful journey by workshops, traditional class and course methodologies.

Currently, engaged in spreading the knowledge of Colours and Art, globally with the aid of technology and expanding my horizons to the global audience.
